Level 1- Story

Doctor Polybius: Through the years, Agent X has gained a reputation as an unorthodox investigator. In our dimension they may be following some seemingly mundane and unconnected task—eating from a bowl that isn’t there, straining to hear sounds no one else can, or recalling memories from a life they never lived. Meanwhile in another reality Agent X’s counterpart braves a hurricane to reach the ancient library that’s home to a spell-casting robot chef!
